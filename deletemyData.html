Delete My Account & Data

You can delete your account directly inside the app, or submit a request below and we'll process it within 7 business days.

⚡ Fastest: Delete in the app

  1. Open Evenly and go to your Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the bottom — tap "Delete Account & Data"
  3. Confirm with your password — done instantly
or submit a request

You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.
Requests are handled within 7 business days.

What gets deleted

Data Status
Account credentials (email & password) Deleted immediately
Profile info (name, photo) Deleted immediately
Payment info (UPI ID, bank details) Deleted immediately
Push notification tokens Deleted immediately
Expenses in shared groups Anonymised — "Deleted User"
Settlement records Retained for group integrity

Shared expense records are anonymised to preserve accurate balances for the remaining group members. If you need these removed too, select the option above or mention it in the notes field.
