Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9 March 2026  |  Last Updated: 9 March 2026

1. Introduction

Evenly ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that helps you split expenses with friends, roommates, and travel companions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"), available on iOS and Android.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and password. Authentication is handled securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Information: Display name and any optional profile details you choose to provide.
  • Expense Data: Descriptions, amounts, categories, split details, and notes attached to expenses you create within groups.
  • Group Data: Group names, custom icons, member lists, and associated expenses and settlements.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in group chat (Premium feature).
  • Settlement Data: Records of payments, settlement requests, and completion status.
  • Payment Proof Photos: Images you upload as proof of payment when settling up (Premium feature).
  • Receipts: Photos of receipts you scan for expense entry (Premium feature). These images are processed for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract description and amount.
  • Feedback & Support: Any information you provide when contacting us for support or reporting bugs.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent, crash logs, and performance data collected through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens required to deliver push notifications for expenses, settlements, friend requests, and reminders.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

  • Phone Contacts: With your explicit permission, we access your device contacts to suggest friends you may want to invite. We only read contact names and email addresses for matching purposes. We do not upload or store your full contact list on our servers.

The following table summarizes the data we collect, whether it is shared, and the purpose:

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide & maintain the App: Create your account, manage groups, track expenses, process settlements, and deliver notifications.
  • Enable social features: Allow friend requests, group membership, contact-based suggestions, and in-group chat.
  • Process expense data: Calculate splits (equal, percentage, exact amounts, custom ratios), optimize debt settlement, and generate analytics dashboards.
  • Receipt scanning: Use OCR to extract information from receipt photos to pre-fill expense details.
  • Send notifications: Notify you of new expenses, settlement requests, friend requests, and payment reminders.
  • Analytics & improvements: Understand how users interact with the App to fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Export reports: Generate CSV exports of your group expense history when requested.
  • Display advertisements: For free-tier users, we may show non-personalized ads. Premium users enjoy an ad-free experience.
  • Customer support: Respond to your inquiries, bug reports, and feedback.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With group members: Your name, expenses, settlements, chat messages, and payment proofs are visible to members of the same group. This is essential for the App's core functionality.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (by Google) for authentication, database, storage, analytics, crashlytics, and cloud messaging. Firebase processes data on our behalf under Google's privacy terms.
  • Advertising partners: For free-tier users, we may use third-party ad networks to display ads. These networks may collect device identifiers for ad delivery. Premium users are not shown ads and no ad-related data is shared.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety & enforcement: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Evenly, its users, or the public.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase Authentication handles secure credential management.
  • Photos (receipts, payment proofs) are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with access restricted to authorized group members.
  • We implement Firebase Security Rules to ensure users can only access data they are authorized to view.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Expense & settlement data: Retained as long as the group exists and your account is active.
  • Chat messages: Retained as long as the group exists.
  • Photos: Retained until the associated expense or settlement is deleted, or your account is deleted.
  • Analytics & crash data: Retained per Firebase's standard retention policies (typically up to 14 months).

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & portability: You can export your group expense data as a CSV file (Premium feature) at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information within the App.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@evenly.app.
  • Contacts permission: You can revoke the contacts permission at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function without this permission; you will simply not see contact-based friend suggestions.
  • Camera permission: You can revoke camera access through your device settings. Receipt scanning and payment proof features will be unavailable without this permission.
  • Push notifications: You can disable push notifications through your device settings.
  • Advertising opt-out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Advertising on iOS; Settings > Google > Ads on Android).

9. Children's Privacy

Evenly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as promptly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@evenly.app so we can take appropriate action.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these countries. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We may also send a push notification for significant changes.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
